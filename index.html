Triangle Area

The formula for the area of triangle is: \(A = \frac{1}{2} \times base \times height\)

Where:

  • \(A\) = area of triangle
  • \(base\) = length of the base
  • \(height\) = distance from the base to the opposite vertex (perpendicular)

A = 1/2 x base x height
